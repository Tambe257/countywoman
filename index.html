The providers and staff at Dr. Ingrid Warmuth Skin Care Center are dedicated to working together as a caring, dynamic team to help manage all of your skin, nail and hair issues through education, diagnosis and individualized treatment. They specialize in high quality dermatologic care in our Elmer and Swedesboro offices and provide up to date information and treatments for various skin conditions, such as acne, warts, psoriasis, rosacea, rashes, precancerous and cancerous lesions and offers comprehensive full body exams, while also providing the latest modern cosmetic medical procedures as well as cyst removals.
